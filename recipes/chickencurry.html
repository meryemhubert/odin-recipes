Chicken Curry

Description

This is a cheap recipe with a ton of flavors! You can serve the chicken curry on a bed of basmati rice or the rice of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts
  • 1 onion
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 chunk of fresh ginger
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1 lemon
  • soy sauce
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • curry powder
  • coriander powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • fresh cilantro

Instructions

  1. Dice the onion.
  2. Grate the garlic.
  3. Cut the chicken breasts into strips.
  4. Put the garlic and the chicken in a mixing bowl.
  5. Drizzle a bit of olive oil.
  6. Add all the spices.
  7. Mix and reserve.
  8. In a pan with a bit of olive oil, cook the diced onion.
  9. Add the chicken mix into the pan and continue cooking.
  10. When the chicken is cooked through, add the coconut milk to the pan and let it reduce until you have a nice creamy sauce consistency.
  11. Add the lemon juice to the pan and turn off the heat.
  12. This is optional, but you can add chopped cilantro to your dish.
